Help

REGISTERING AND SIGNING UP


BAlert requires you to have an active Email and phone number to start setting up your account.


  • To get started, ensure that your internet connection is stable and strong
  • After entering all your details, you will receive a confirmation Email that your account has been successfully setup


    • LOGGING AN ALERT


    After logging in, you shall be directed to the Alert page, where you will report the crime.


    • In the alert box, type in your alert or crime, click "ALERT" and the crime will immediately be reported to the police.



    BAlert takes your privacy seriously and you are assured that your information will not be shared outside our website.


  • When you report a crime on the site, your identity remains anonymous.



    • We would love to hear your feedback about our website and should you also require further assistance, contact us on +27 61 023 1809 or email us on customer@balert.org.



    The first step to troubleshooting is making sure you have a stable and secure internet connection.


    • If you cannot log into your account, check your connection and also ensure that you have entered the correct password annd username.
    • In the case that you have forgotten your password, DON'T PANIC, click on "Forgot password" and reset your password or username. An email will be sent to you confirming your new details.
    • Page not loading? Refresh the page and if the problem persists, close your browser and try logging in again.
