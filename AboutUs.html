About Us

WHAT DO WE DO?

With the rising crime rate in our communities, we all fear for our safety. We are a site that takes the safety of people seriously.

Are you a victim of crime, have you witnessed a crime in your commumnity and you could not get the word out there?

BAlert is here for you, providing members of communities and victims of crime the platform to report crimes anonymously. We also provide communities with the opportunity to unite and create preventative measures to the crimes happening around them.

WHY DO WE DO WHAT WE DO?

That feeling of fear when you walk alone in the street, the crippling feeling of confusion and shock that washes over you when you witness a crime, or when you are a victim of crime and nobody is there to help you. We can relate all too well.

We at BAlert know how it feels to feel unsafe and we dedicate ourselves to helping communities to find ways to keep each other safe and informed.

Think of us as a middleman between yourself and your neighbours.

Everybody deserves to feel safe and to be aware of what goes on around them.

SAFETY, UNITY AND AWARENESS - THAT'S WHY WE ARE HERE.
